Who We Are

Built to bring
world-class tech
to Ethiopia

China produces some of the world's most capable enterprise technology — yet many African markets remain underserved due to the absence of professional, trusted local distribution.

Lucy Technology was founded to close that gap. Incorporated in Hong Kong, operating in Ethiopia, we specialize in bringing Ruijie networking equipment, enterprise-grade batteries, and Dahua surveillance systems to B2B clients across the country.
